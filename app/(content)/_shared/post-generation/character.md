# Post Generation Strategy: Character TopicPages
# Used by: scripts/ai-topic-post-bot.mjs

---

## 1. Character Verdict
Simple high-signal opinions about the character as a whole.

- "Is [Character] a well-written character?" → One of the best / Pretty good / Average / Poorly written
- "Hero, villain, or something in between?" → Clear hero / Clear villain / Morally grey / Changes throughout
- "Did [Character] get the ending they deserved?" → Absolutely / Not quite / They deserved better / They deserved worse
- "Overrated or underrated?" → Criminally underrated / Fairly rated / A bit overrated / Very overrated
- "Did [Character]'s arc pay off?" → Completely / Mostly / It was rushed / There was no real arc

---

## 2. Loyalty & Alignment
Get users to place themselves relative to the character.

- "Would you trust [Character] with your life?" → Without question / Maybe / Probably not / Absolutely not
- "Would you side with [Character] or against them?" → With them / Against them / Depends on the situation
- "Are they a good role model?" → Yes, genuinely / In some ways / Not really / Terrible role model
- "Would you want [Character] as a friend?" → Yes! / Depends on the day / Probably not / They'd be exhausting
- "If [Character] switched sides, would you follow?" → Immediately / Hesitantly / No / Already wish they had

---

## 3. Power & Ability Debates
For characters from action, fantasy, or sci-fi universes.

- "Is [Character] overpowered?" → Way too OP / Balanced / Could be stronger / Definitely underpowered
- "Best ability [Character] has?" → list of their key abilities/powers
- "In a fight with [Character B], who wins?" → [Character A] / [Character B] / Depends on conditions / Draw
- "What power would you take from [Character] if you could?" → list of their abilities
- "Is [Character]'s power well-utilized in the story?" → Perfect use / Could've been more / Wasted entirely

---

## 4. Relationships & Dynamics
For characters known for their bonds, rivalries, or romances.

- "Best relationship [Character] had?" → list of key relationships (friend, rival, love interest)
- "Did [Character] treat [Person] right?" → Yes, completely / Mostly / Not really / Absolutely not
- "Was [Character]'s rivalry with [Character B] the best part of the show/game?" → Yes / One of the highlights / Preferred other dynamics / Overplayed
- "Should [Character] have ended up with [love interest]?" → Yes, perfect match / No, wrong choice / Didn't need a romance
- "Who brought out the best in [Character]?" → list of supporting characters

---

## 5. Fan Debate Zone
The hottest, most divisive character opinions.

- "Most controversial thing [Character] did — justified or not?" → Completely justified / Had some logic / Hard to say / Never justified
- "Did [Character] deserve redemption?" → Yes, earned it / Partially / Not really / They shouldn't have been redeemed
- "Is [Character] the best in their universe?" → Easily / Top tier / Mid-range / No
- "Should [Character] have died when they did?" → Yes, perfect moment / No, too soon / No, should've died earlier / They shouldn't have died at all
- "Was [Character] done justice by the writers?" → Yes / Mostly / Could've been better / Completely wasted

---

## 6. Just-for-Fun / Light Engagement
Lower-stakes posts that any fan can quickly answer.

- "One word to describe [Character]?" → list of strong adjectives (Iconic / Tragic / Annoying / Underrated / Chaotic)
- "Which [Character] era was the best?" → Early / Mid / Late / They peaked at the end
- "If [Character] had a different profession in real life, what would it be?" → fun role options
- "Favourite [Character] outfit/look?" → key visual eras or forms
- "Would [Character] survive in the real world?" → Thriving / Doing okay / Struggling / Completely lost

---

## Generation Rules for AI Prompt (Character)
- Use Character Verdict for main protagonists or iconic villains.
- Use Loyalty & Alignment for morally grey or divisive characters (anti-heroes work best).
- Use Power & Ability for characters from action/fantasy/sci-fi genres only.
- Use Relationships & Dynamics for characters known more for their bonds than their battles.
- Use Fan Debate Zone for highly controversial characters where fandom is split (e.g. Eren Yeager, Skyler White, Cersei Lannister).
- Use Just-for-Fun for beloved but non-controversial characters to keep energy light.
- Always refer to the character by name, not just "the protagonist" or "the main character".
