# Gamification for Fandoms: Use Cases for the `/characters` Path

The `/characters` path (e.g., `/characters/spider-man`, `/characters/naruto`) acts as a dedicated hub for specific fictional personalities. By combining this path with the different **Wheel Types**, it transforms from a simple static wiki page into a highly interactive community playground for fandoms.

Here is how users will leverage the different wheel types within the `/characters` ecosystem:

---

## 1. The Default Wheel (Roleplay, Debates & Fan Engagement)
**Concept:** The standard, no-stakes randomizer used for fandom fun and social sharing.

### User Scenarios:
*   **"Which Version Are You?":** A personality-style wheel on the `/characters/batman` page. Users spin to see if they are Adam West, Christian Bale, Robert Pattinson, or LEGO Batman today, and share the result on social media.
*   **Shipping & Matchmaking:** A user creates a "Who should [Character] date?" wheel. Fans spin to randomly pair the character with other characters to spark (or settle) fandom debates.
*   **"Who Would Win?":** A matchup generator. You land on the `/characters/goku` page, spin the "Opponent Wheel," and it lands on Superman, prompting a discussion in the comments underneath the wheel.

---

## 2. The Quiz Wheel (Lore & Deep Trivia)
**Concept:** A wheel that tests hardcore fandom knowledge about that specific character.

### User Scenarios:
*   **Quote Attribution:** "Did [Character] really say this?" A spin triggers a famous or obscure quote, and the user must identify the exact manga chapter, movie scene, or comic issue it appeared in.
*   **Voice Actor (Seiyuu) Trivia:** Landing on a specific anime character prompts a quiz about their voice actor, testing if the user knows what other famous roles they have voiced.
*   **Abilities & Weaknesses:** Users test their knowledge on the specific cooldown times of a character's ability in a game like *Overwatch*, or the specific conditions required for a character's transformation in an anime.

---

## 3. The Challenge Wheel (Creative Prompts & Fan Works)
**Concept:** Wheels that issue real-world challenges for artists, cosplayers, and dedicated fans to complete and verify.

### User Scenarios:
*   **The Art Prompt Challenge:** An artist visits `/characters/sailor-moon` and spins an "Art Challenge" wheel. It assigns them: *"Draw this character in a Cyberpunk aesthetic."* It goes into their "Pending" tab, they draw it over the week, and verify later (potentially uploading a link to their artwork).
*   **The Cosplay Roulette:** A cosplayer spins to decide which niche, alternate outfit of a character they are going to build for the next convention (e.g., "Spinning for Spider-Man: Lands on Spider-Man Noir"). 
*   **Reading/Watch Guides:** A wheel containing the "Top 10 Essential Comic Arcs" for a superhero. Fans spin to see which arc they should read next, then come back to pass the verification quiz.

---

## 4. The Content Wheel (Interactive Profiles & Timelines)
**Concept:** A wheel that acts as an interactive showcase of the character's history.

### User Scenarios:
*   **Evolution of a Character:** A wheel on `/characters/iron-man` displaying all his different armor suits. Spinning lands on "Hulkbuster." The pop-up card shows the suit's first appearance, its stats, and the movie/comic it featured in.
*   **Relationship Trees:** Spin a segment containing an allied or rival character. The content card explains the exact history and relationship dynamics between the main character and the spun character.
