# SpinWheel: Use Cases for a General Audience

SpinWheel is a fun, interactive, and community-driven decision-making platform. Whether you are struggling with everyday choices, organizing an event, or looking for crowdsourced advice, SpinWheel makes deciding effortless and entertaining.

Here are the most popular ways to use SpinWheel:

## 1. Everyday Decision Making (The Classic Spin)
* **What's for Dinner?**: Can't agree on takeout? Put Pizza, Sushi, Burgers, and Tacos on a wheel and let fate decide.
* **Movie Night Roulette**: Stop scrolling through streaming apps forever. Put your watchlist on a wheel and spin it.
* **Weekend Activities**: Indoors or outdoors? Hiking, museum, or lazy day at home? Take the friction out of planning.

## 2. Community Advice & Dilemmas ("Ask" Feature)
* **Shopping Dilemmas**: "Which laptop should I buy? Mac or PC?" Post it to the Ask feed and let real people vote on your dilemma.
* **Life & Relationship Advice**: "Should I text them first?" Break the tie by letting the community weigh in on your situation.
* **Fashion Choices**: "Which outfit should I wear to the wedding?" Let the wisdom of the crowd guide your styling.

## 3. Party Games & Social Gatherings
* **Truth or Dare**: Create a wheel packed with custom truths and dares for your next house party.
* **Drinking Games**: "Take 2 sips," "Give a sip," or "Everyone drinks." 
* **Who Pays the Bill?**: Out with friends? Put everyone's name on the wheel to find out who picks up the tab.

## 4. Classroom & Education
* **Random Student Picker**: Teachers can put their students' names on a wheel to fairly select who presents next or answers a question.
* **Interactive Quizzes (MCQ Wheels)**: Create educational wheels where each segment asks a multiple-choice question or True/False prompt to make learning engaging.
* **Reward Wheels**: Spin to give out classroom perks (e.g., 5 minutes extra recess, choose your seat).

## 5. Productivity & Work
* **Task Prioritization**: Overwhelmed by a massive to-do list? Put your tasks on a wheel and commit to doing whatever it lands on first.
* **Meeting Icebreakers**: "Share a fun fact," "Two truths and a lie," or "What are you watching right now?" great for kicking off remote team meetings.
* **Agile/Scrum Roles**: Randomly select who runs the daily stand-up meeting.

## 6. Goal Tracking & Habits (User Dashboard)
* **Workout Roulette**: Leg day, cardio, yoga, or rest day? Make fitness unpredictable and fun.
* **Building a Decision Streak**: Use the dashboard to track how many decisions you make every day, build a streak, and review your most common outcomes.

## 7. Content Creators & Streamers
* **Interactive Streams**: Twitch or YouTube streamers can use the wheel to decide what game to play next, or what challenge to do based on chat suggestions.
* **Giveaways**: Put subscriber names on the wheel to easily draw a random winner live on camera.
