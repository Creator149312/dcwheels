# Gamification for Entertainment: Use Cases for Movie & Anime Lovers

The transition to distinct **Wheel Types** transforms the platform from a simple "what to watch" decider into a community-driven entertainment tracking and trivia hub. Here is how cinephiles and anime fans will use the new tools:

---

## 1. The Default Wheel (Indecision Killers & Roulettes)
**Concept:** The standard, no-stakes randomizer.

### User Scenarios:
*   **"What Are We Watching Tonight?":** A group of friends populates a wheel with their respective watchlists. One spin decides the movie, ending the infamous 45-minute scroll through Netflix.
*   **Episodic Roulette:** Fans of episodic comedies (like *The Office*, *Friends*, or *Gintama*) create wheels filled with episode numbers. A spin decides which random standalone episode they re-watch.
*   **Genre/Trope Selectors:** "I want to watch anime, but I don't know what kind." Spin a wheel of tropes (Isekai, Mecha, Slice of Life, Cyberpunk) to pick the vibe for the evening.

---

## 2. The Quiz Wheel (Fandom Trivia)
**Concept:** Landing on a segment triggers a trivia question related to the spin.

### User Scenarios:
*   **Lore Master Battles:** A "Lord of the Rings" or "Naruto" trivia wheel. Landing on "Shire" or "Hidden Leaf" prompts a devastatingly hard lore question to test who the biggest fan in the Discord call actually is.
*   **"Name That Tune" (Anime OPs):** A segment lands on an anime title, and the quiz asks the user to correctly identify the artist who performed the Opening theme song.
*   **Quote Matching:** Spin to select a famous character, and the pop-up asks the user to correctly identify their famous quote from a multiple-choice list.

---

## 3. The Challenge Wheel (Backlog Clearing & Watch Pacts)
**Concept:** A spin assigns a specific piece of media. The user watches it over hours/days, then returns to pass a verification quiz and earn a badge.

### User Scenarios:
*   **The Oscar Nominee Run:** A wheel populated exclusively with Best Picture winners. A user spins and gets assigned *Parasite*. It stays in their "Pending" tab. They watch it over the weekend, come back, take the verification quiz to prove they paid attention, and earn the "Kino Snob" badge.
*   **The "Big Three" Anime Challenge:** A massive challenge wheel for finishing long-running Shonen arcs. Spin to commit to watching the "Soul Society Arc." Once completed, verify and earn the badge.
*   **Clearing the Watchlist:** Users use challenge wheels to force themselves to finally watch the movies they've been putting off. The gamified reward at the end provides the necessary motivation.

---

## 4. The Content Wheel (Lore & Discovery)
**Concept:** A wheel that serves as an interactive wiki or recommendation engine.

### User Scenarios:
*   **Character Discoveries:** A wheel of *Marvel Cinematic Universe* characters. Spinning "Wanda Maximoff" opens a rich card with brief backstories, essential comic reading lists, and her movie timeline.
*   **Curated Recommendations:** A wheel titled "Top 10 Hidden Gem Sci-Fi Movies." Spinning reveals a movie poster, a quick synopsis, where it's streaming, and why the creator recommends it.
